summarize

Description

Aggregates on the entire DataFrame without groups. The resulting DataFrame will also contain the grouping columns.

Usage

## S4 method for signature 'GroupedData'
agg(x, ...)

## S4 method for signature 'GroupedData'
summarize(x, ...)

Arguments

x

a GroupedData

Details

Value

a DataFrame

Examples
